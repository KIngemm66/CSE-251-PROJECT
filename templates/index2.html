    About Us

    UgoLibs is a library database system. It is an online searchable electronic index of published, reliable resources that provide access to a wealth of useful research materials from academic journals, books, magazines and e-books. We buy subscriptions to worldwide databases of schools and universities so that learners and readers like you can find useful and interesting information. Our database library system is intended to automate library activities such as creating a new borrower, giving books to the borrowers, maintaining the details of all the items that were available in the books.
